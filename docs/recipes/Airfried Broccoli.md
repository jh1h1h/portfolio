## Ingredients
Serves: 2
1 head of broccoli
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon light soy sauce
pepper

## Steps
1. Wash broccoli and cut to the size u wanna eat, like reasonable size
2. Mix the broccoli and soy sauce and oil THOROUGHLY, try and make sure can kinda see all the individual broccoli pieces covered in oil
3. Airfry at 180C for 4-5mins
4. Add pepper at the end if u want
