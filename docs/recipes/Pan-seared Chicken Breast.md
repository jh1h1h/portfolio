## Ingredients
Serves: 1
1 chicken breast
1/8 cup flour (or self-raising flour)
1/4 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon chicken seasoning
1/2 tablespoon olive oil
pepper

## Steps
1. Pat dry the chicken breast with kitchen towel until can see that its dry but no need fully dry
2. Slice the chicken horizontally if too thick
3. Mix the flour, salt, and chicken seasoning
4. Lather the breast inside the mix, try to make sure they all visibly got powder
5. Add pepper to the chicken
6. Heat up oil into the pan, hot but not sizzling
7. Cook chicken, 4-5mins each side, medium-ish heat, internal temp should reach 74C
