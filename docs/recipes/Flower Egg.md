## Ingredients
Serves: 1

2 eggs<br>
oil (so far i've used olive)<br>
salt<br>
non-stick frying pan

## Steps
1. Beat 2 eggs
2. Heat frying pan on high heat, then add and heat oil
3. Pour in the eggs, once u see a solid layer forming at the bottom, drag the spatula from one side and fold the egg to the direct opposite side, making sure not to tear the egg.
4. Once reach the other side, go to the corner of the egg and fold/curl it towards the centre.
5. Repeat until all the egg has been cooked and it looks like a flower!
